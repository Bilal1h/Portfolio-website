My story in a nutshell.


**Hey there! **

I'm Muhammad Bilal Haider, a 10th-grade student with a passion for design and development that burns brighter than a supernova.✨

My journey began 3 years ago when I dove headfirst into the world of graphic design. Photoshop and Illustrator were my playgrounds, and I crafted logos, banners, and business cards like a seasoned artist.

By the end of 2020, I wasn't just making samples, I was taking on real clients. Dealing with their needs and exceeding their expectations became my new playground.

But my creative thirst wouldn't be quenched by pixels alone. Mid-2021 saw me dive into web development, mastering the holy trinity of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Frameworks and libraries became my new tools, and I eagerly built intricate projects that pushed the boundaries of what I thought possible.

But wait, there's more! My insatiable curiosity led me to Python, a language that captivated me with its power and versatility. From scripts to GUIs, I poured my passion into creating projects that showcased the magic of code. And my crowning achievement? An AI software that generates videos from scratch – a testament to my ever-evolving skillset.
